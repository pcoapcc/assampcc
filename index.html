Welcome to Assam Pradesh Congress Committee (APCC)

Standing with the People of Assam

Assam Pradesh Congress Committee was formed in June 1921. Its headquarters is at Rajiv Bhavan in GS Road Guwahati. Kuladhar Chaliha was the first elected president and Chabilal Upadhyaya was the first selected president of the committee when it was founded.
